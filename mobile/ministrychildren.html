Children's Ministry serves and ministers to children and their families from birth until they are ready to enter middle school. We are here to teach kids about Jesus and God’s plan of redemption through Old and New Testament scripture, and encourage them to rely on God through every circumstance of their lives. We want the children of our church to understand the immense love of God and learn how to establish an individual relationship with Him. We hope to build up future leaders of our faith so that many more may learn of God’s grace through their example and testimony.
