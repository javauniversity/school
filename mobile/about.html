Pastor’s Welcome

In 2004, my wife, Cynthia, and I began leading Bible studies. As we think about the beautiful evolution of what has become of Christ Our King Community Church, one word comes to mind: awe. The Lord is the author of our story, and we exist to build broken people into blessed people who live, learn, and lead together as a grace filled community. That includes me, as well as my wife. Growing together with a community is one of the most beautiful parts of the human experience.

The beauty of grace will never be fully grasped without the knowledge of truth of how broken and powerless we are without our Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ. His death, burial, resurrection remind us that He did what we could not do. He became the final sacrifice, allowing us to enter into a relationship with our Father. As we walk through each day, loving our neighbor, accepting grace and extending grace, we are showing the world what it looks like when Christ is King in every aspect of our lives from the sanctuary to the business meeting to the dinner table.

My desire is to see a community that knows the true identity of Jesus, and it starts with knowing God’s infallible Word. Not the words that we have seen abbreviated in journals or paraphrased on television, but the word of God from the holy Bible. One of the things I take most seriously is communicating God’s truth and the timeless nature of the Bible. It is a beautiful story of our Holy God and his intentional plan for salvation. It does not need updates, edits or abbreviations.

As you visit, you will hear my passion for living a life where the pulpit meets the pavement. My prayer is for Christ Our King Community Church to be an empowered army of people regularly showing their faith.
