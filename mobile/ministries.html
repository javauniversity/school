Children

Children's Ministry serves and ministers to children and their families from birth until they are ready to enter middle school. We are here to teach kids about Jesus and God’s plan of redemption through Old and New Testament scripture, and encourage them to rely on God through every circumstance of their lives. We want the children of our church to understand the immense love of God and learn how to establish an individual relationship with Him. We hope to build up future leaders of our faith so that many more may learn of God’s grace through their example and testimony.

Praise/Dance

This ministry is designed to encourage God people and destroy yokes of bondage upon the congregation through worshiping God in song and dance. Our expression of worship through song and dance will be used to bring deliverance, give breakthrough, and break down strongholds in both the saved and unsaved lives. We aim to use our bodies as vessels of the Holy Spirit that flows through us to visually express praise and worship of Christ who is Our King

Dads

DADs, Determined to Achieve my Destiny, provides foundational teachings, interactions and events for young men that develop wisdom, develop character and always honor Christ. while living out their destiny according to God’s will for their lives. Jeremiah 29:11 For I know the plans I have for your, says the Lord. They are plans for good and not for evil, to give you a future and hope.

Jewels

Our mission is to teach, encourage and equip young girls of the COKCC family to become disciplined young women of grace and virtue by having self-control, making wise decisions and growing in their commitment to Christ.

IRON MEN

At COKCC we believe that the only way a man can avoid being spiritually bankrupt is by being connected to the one who is spiritually rich in the Lord Jesus Christ. It is the desire of the men's ministry of COKCC to see men move from brokenness to blessedness so that on a consistent basis men can demonstrate that Jesus is King in every area of their live

Soul Sister

The purpose of Soul Sisters, the women's ministry at Christ Our King Community, is to provide an environment where women connect deeply at the soul, thus becoming kindred sisters. It Is our commitment to encourage one another to move from brokenness to blessedness. Our ministry strives to encourage and provide rich community experiences through an intimate connection with Christ our Heavenly King, that leads to lasting transformation. We provide a variety of opportunities for women of all ages to meet, connect and grow through bible studies, special events, conferences, retreats and community outreaches.

Small Group

At COKCC, we strive to grow deeper in community through joining together in small groups. These groups gather to grow in their knowledge of God and his Word, to love and care for one another as community, and to share the good news of Jesus with their neighbors and friends. Our small groups typically meet in a home and consist of a time of bible study, sharing, fellowship and prayer. Being an active part of a small group is one of the best ways to grow in Christ with the support of fellow believers, and to get connected with others. Small Groups usually have 10-20 members and meet to study God's Word, apply it to life, have fellowship, and pray for each other.
